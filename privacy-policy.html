Privacy Policy

Block Gems | Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Block Gems collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you play the game, use online features, view ads, or request account deletion.

We do not sell personal and sensitive user data. We use player data to run the game, save progress, support leaderboards, provide account features, process deletion requests, and show ads.

Information We Collect

  • Account information, such as email address, display name, username, authentication ID, and anonymous guest ID.
  • Game progress, such as score, level, XP, coins, B-Coins, gems, boosters, inventory items, unlocked cosmetics, quests, achievements, streak rewards, and settings.
  • Leaderboard information, such as display name, score, rankings, and related gameplay statistics.
  • Local device storage data used to keep the game playable offline and restore progress.
  • Account deletion request data, such as email address, display name, reason for deletion, request timestamp, and request status.
  • Advertising and app interaction data handled by Google AdMob, including data used to serve banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads.
  • Technical and security information processed by platform or service providers, such as crash, diagnostic, fraud-prevention, app integrity, and device or advertising identifiers where applicable.

How We Use Information

  • To create and maintain player accounts.
  • To save and sync game progress between local storage and cloud storage.
  • To display leaderboards, rankings, rewards, inventory, store items, and game statistics.
  • To provide rewarded ads and grant in-game rewards after eligible ad views.
  • To detect abuse, protect game integrity, and operate cloud services securely.
  • To keep the app reliable, diagnose technical issues, and comply with platform, legal, and security requirements.
  • To process account and data deletion requests.

Third-Party Services

Block Gems uses third-party services that may collect or process data according to their own policies:

  • Firebase Authentication for sign-in and account identity.
  • Firebase Firestore and Realtime Database for cloud save, profile data, leaderboard data, admin checks, and deletion requests.
  • Google AdMob for advertising, including rewarded ads, interstitial ads, and banner ads.
  • Google Play services and Android platform services required for app distribution, security, and platform operation.

Data Sharing

We share data only as needed to operate the app, provide cloud save and advertising services, comply with law, protect users, or process account deletion requests. Data may be processed by Google services such as Firebase and AdMob. We do not sell personal and sensitive user data.

Advertising

Block Gems shows ads through Google AdMob. Ads may use device or advertising identifiers and app interaction data. Rewarded ads are optional and are used to grant in-game rewards after the ad is completed. Users in regions requiring consent may be shown consent options where applicable.

Data Safety Summary

The information in this policy is intended to match the Google Play Data safety form for Block Gems. Depending on how you use the app, the app may collect or process account information, app activity, app information and performance data, device or other identifiers, and gameplay progress. Data may be shared with service providers such as Google Firebase, Google AdMob, Google Play services, and Android platform services to provide app functionality, analytics, security, fraud prevention, advertising, and legal compliance.

Storage and Security

Game data may be stored locally on your device and synced to Firebase cloud services. Data is transmitted using encrypted connections where supported by the platform and service providers. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we use reasonable technical measures to protect player data.

Retention and Deletion

We keep account and gameplay data while your account is active or as needed to provide game features. You can request account and data deletion from inside the app or through the public deletion page. When deletion is processed, account data and associated cloud profile data are removed unless retention is required for security, legal, or abuse-prevention reasons. Local data stored on your device may also be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

Account deletion page: delete-account.html

Your Privacy Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data under regulations like the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in Europe and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). These rights include the right to access, correct, or request the permanent deletion of your personal data. You can exercise your right to delete data by using our public deletion page linked below.

Children

Block Gems is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent required by applicable law. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it where required.

International Users

Block Gems may be available in multiple countries or regions. By using online features, your data may be processed by service providers in countries where they operate, subject to their security and privacy controls and applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when game features, services, legal requirements, or data practices change. The latest version will be posted on this page with the updated date.
